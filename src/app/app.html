Retro Poker 60-2699

This is a 1:1 recreation of the classic RadioShack handheld poker game, following the "Jacks or Better" draw poker variant.

How to Play

  • Ante: At the beginning of every round, you ante 5 points.
  • Start: Each player starts the game with 100 points.
  • Deal: Press DEAL to see your first 5 cards.
  • Hold: Tap cards you want to keep (marked as "HELD").
  • Draw: Press DRAW to replace unheld cards and see if you won!

Paytable

Royal Flush5000 pts
Straight Flush250 pts
Four of a Kind125 pts
Full House40 pts
Flush25 pts
Straight20 pts
Three of a Kind15 pts
Two Pair10 pts
Pair (Jacks+)5 pts

* Note: Pairs of 10s or lower award 0 points.
